Last updated: 15 July 2026

NexMoney Ltd ("NexMoney", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

NexMoney is a trading style of NexMoney Ltd, an Appointed Representative of Stonebridge Mortgage Solutions Ltd, which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FRN 610875). NexMoney Ltd is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

What we collect

To provide mortgage and insurance advice we may collect: your name and contact details; date of birth; employment and income details; outgoings and financial commitments; credit history; details of the property; identification documents (for anti-money-laundering checks); and, where relevant to protection insurance, limited health information (special category data), which we process only with your explicit consent.

Why we process it

We use your data to assess your circumstances and recommend suitable mortgage and protection products (performance of a contract); to submit applications to lenders and insurers on your instruction; to meet our legal and regulatory obligations, including FCA rules and anti-money-laundering law; and, with your consent, to keep in touch about products relevant to you (for example when your fixed rate is due to expire). You can withdraw marketing consent at any time.

Who we share it with

Where necessary to provide our service, we share your data with: lenders and insurers you apply to; Stonebridge Mortgage Solutions Ltd, our principal firm, for compliance oversight; credit reference agencies; conveyancers and surveyors involved in your transaction; and our regulated IT and administration providers. We never sell your personal data.

How long we keep it

We retain client records for as long as required by FCA rules and applicable law — typically a minimum of six years after our relationship ends, and indefinitely for certain mortgage and pension-related advice records where regulation requires.

Your rights

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to have inaccurate data corrected, to request erasure, to restrict or object to processing, to data portability, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise any of these rights, email info@nexmoney.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, please contact us first so we can put it right. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or on 0303 123 1113.
