Cookie Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. This policy explains which cookies nexmoney.co.uk uses and how you can control them.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary cookies. These are required for the website to function — for example, remembering your cookie preferences. They cannot be switched off and store no personally identifiable information.

Analytics cookies. Where enabled, we use analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are popular and how people find us. This data is aggregated and does not identify you directly. Analytics cookies are only set with your consent.

We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Third-party cookies

Some pages load resources from third parties (for example Google Fonts). These providers may set their own cookies; we do not control them. Please refer to the relevant provider's privacy policy for details.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Guides for the most common browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect how the site works.

More information

For details of how we handle personal data more generally, see our Privacy Policy. Questions? Email info@nexmoney.co.uk.
