All Our Services

Mortgages are just the beginning. We offer a full range of financial services to protect your property, your family, and your future.

Protection

Protect what
matters most

Your mortgage is probably your biggest financial commitment. So what happens if you can't work due to illness, or something worse? Protection insurance ensures your family and home are safe — whatever life throws at you.

Many lenders require some form of protection when you take out a mortgage. We'll advise on the right level of cover for your circumstances, and find you the most competitive premiums from across the market.

Life Insurance

Pays out a lump sum to your family if you die during the policy term. Typically designed to pay off the mortgage so your family can stay in their home.

Critical Illness Insurance

Pays a tax-free lump sum if you're diagnosed with a serious illness such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke. Gives you financial breathing room when you need it most.

Income Protection

Provides a monthly income if you're unable to work due to illness or injury — so you can keep up your mortgage repayments and other household bills.

General Insurance

Protect your property

Your home is your biggest asset. Make sure it's properly protected.

Buildings Insurance

Required by most mortgage lenders, buildings insurance covers the structure of your home against damage from fire, flood, storms, subsidence, and more. We find competitive quotes tailored to your property.

Get a quote

Contents Insurance

Covers your personal belongings inside the home — furniture, electronics, clothing, and valuables — against theft, accidental damage, and more. Often bundled with buildings cover for better value.

Get a quote

ASU Insurance (Accident, Sickness & Unemployment)

Covers your mortgage repayments if you're unable to work due to accident, sickness, or involuntary redundancy. Peace of mind that your home is safe even in difficult times.

Get a quote
