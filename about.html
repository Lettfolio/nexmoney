Our Story

More than a
mortgage broker

NexMoney was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves honest, expert mortgage advice — not a one-size-fits-all product pushed by a bank.

Based in Bournemouth, Dorset, we've been helping clients across the South of England and the UK navigate the mortgage market for over a decade. From first-time buyers anxious about their first step onto the ladder, to seasoned investors growing their portfolios — we've helped hundreds of clients secure the right deal.

As an Appointed Representative of Stonebridge Mortgage Solutions Ltd, we're fully authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority, giving you peace of mind that your interests always come first.

Our Promise

The NexMoney Promise

These are the commitments we make to every single client, every single time.

Honesty, always

We'll tell you the truth about what's available, what you qualify for, and what we recommend — even if it's not what you wanted to hear.

Prompt, responsive service

We respond quickly. When you call or email, you'll hear back fast — not be left waiting days wondering what's happening with your application.

Dedicated advisor

You'll work with one dedicated advisor throughout your case — not be passed around a call centre or have to repeat yourself constantly.

Whole-of-market access

We don't have preferred lenders or exclusive arrangements that bias our recommendations. We search the whole market to find what's genuinely best for you.

Transparent fees

No surprises. Our typical fee is £375 and we'll tell you upfront — and on many products, there's no broker fee to you at all.

We go the extra mile

We chase lenders, solicitors and estate agents on your behalf. We're available evenings and weekends. We won't rest until the keys are in your hand.
